How to use ApplyReady

Seven steps to go from job listing to verdict and outreach — in under a minute. Follow this once and it becomes second nature.

Step 01

Install the extension

Head to the Chrome Web Store and add ApplyReady to your browser. It takes one click and requires no account or sign-up.

chromewebstore.google.com
Chrome Web Store · ApplyReady install page
✦
After install Pin the extension to your toolbar so the panel is always one click away — click the puzzle icon in Chrome and pin ApplyReady.
Step 02

Paste your resume

Click the extension icon to open the panel, then open Settings. Paste your resume text — skills, experience, education. ApplyReady saves it and uses it for every analysis.

ApplyReady · Settings panel
Settings panel · Resume input field
ℹ
Plain text only Copy your resume as plain text — no need to upload a file. Paste everything: skills, experience, education, and any relevant keywords.
Step 03

Set your visa status

In Settings, select your current authorization — F-1, OPT, STEM OPT, CPT, or needs H-1B. This is what makes every analysis specific to your situation.

ApplyReady · Settings panel
Settings panel · Visa status selector
✦
Also set your city Add your current location so ApplyReady can flag onsite-only roles that would require relocation.
Step 04

Open any job listing

Navigate to any job posting — LinkedIn, Indeed, Handshake, Glassdoor, or a company careers page. ApplyReady works on all of them automatically.

linkedin.com/jobs/view/...
Any job board · ApplyReady detects the listing automatically
ℹ
Selection mode On pages where auto-detect doesn't work, select the job description text with your cursor. A floating Analyze button will appear.
Step 05

Click Analyze

Open the ApplyReady panel and click "Analyze this position." The extension reads the listing, cross-references your profile and visa status, and returns a result in seconds.

ApplyReady · Analyzing…
ApplyReady panel · Analyze button
Step 06

Read your verdict

You'll get a clear APPLY, SKIP, or MAYBE — with a confidence level, a summary, a list of reasons to apply, and any red flags including visa conflicts, location issues, or experience gaps.

ApplyReady · Results
Verdict panel · APPLY / SKIP / MAYBE with breakdown
✦
MAYBE means ask A MAYBE verdict usually means one thing is unclear — sponsorship not confirmed, hybrid policy vague. The red flags section tells you exactly what to ask the recruiter.
Step 07

Draft your outreach

Below the verdict, choose a format — Cover Letter, Email, LinkedIn, or Handshake. Optionally add the recruiter's name. Click Generate, then copy and send.

ApplyReady · Outreach
Outreach panel · Four formats, one click
ℹ
Add the recruiter's name If you found the recruiter on LinkedIn, paste their first name before generating. The message will address them directly instead of "Dear Hiring Manager."

You're ready to apply.

That's the whole workflow — install once, analyze every listing in seconds, send outreach that actually sounds like you.
