01 Getting Started

ApplyReady is a Chrome extension built specifically for international students on F-1, OPT, STEM OPT, CPT, or H-1B visas. It reads any job listing in seconds, flags visa conflicts, sponsorship walls, and location mismatches, then drafts your outreach — so you stop wasting time on roles you can't take.

Yes — completely free during beta. You get 25 job analyses per day at no cost. No credit card, no subscription, no paywall.

No account is required to install and use the extension. You only need to provide your email if you want daily rate limit tracking and outreach generation. Authentication uses a magic link — no password needed.

1. Install from the Chrome Web Store — one click, shows up instantly.

2. Open the extension panel and paste your resume text into Settings.

3. Set your visa status and location once.

4. Open any job listing and click Analyze. Done.

Google Chrome only for now. Firefox, Edge, and Safari support are on the roadmap but not yet available.

02 Visa & Eligibility

Set your status once in Settings — every analysis is personalized to your authorization level.

F-1 OPT STEM OPT CPT Needs H-1B

The extension reads the full listing and looks for language patterns indicating restrictions — phrases like "must be authorized to work", "no sponsorship", "US citizens only", security clearance requirements, and similar signals. It cross-references these against your visa status.

It catches the vast majority of cases visible in the listing itself, though some companies bury restrictions deeper in the application portal.

APPLY — strong match. Your visa status is compatible, skills align, no major red flags found.

SKIP — clear disqualifier. The role explicitly excludes your visa type, requires clearance, or has a hard experience mismatch.

MAYBE — worth a closer look. Sponsorship isn't confirmed but not denied, or there's an ambiguous requirement worth clarifying first.

Yes. Set your city once in Settings. ApplyReady flags roles that are onsite-only in a different city — so "hybrid" in San Francisco doesn't slip past you when you're in Ohio and can't relocate.

05 Privacy & Data

Your resume is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's chrome.storage API. It never leaves your device except when you trigger an analysis — sent over HTTPS and discarded immediately after. Never stored on our servers.

No. ApplyReady does not sell data, does not share it with advertisers, and contains zero ad trackers or third-party analytics. The only external services used are Supabase (authentication) and the Anthropic Claude API (analysis) — neither is used for advertising.

No. ApplyReady does not have access to your browsing history and does not track which pages you visit. It only reads the active page's content when you explicitly click Analyze.

We log your email address (for authentication) and usage records — timestamps of when an analysis was requested, for rate limiting only. We do not log job listing content, your resume, outreach messages, or analysis results.
